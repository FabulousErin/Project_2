USDA Crop Data by State

Click on a state in the map to learn more about the crops produced in the state. The pie chart will show you the top 5 crops produced in the state by value from 2020. The bar chart will display the state's total area planted to field crops over the past 10 years. For the line chart you can choose from the drop down menu of the crops produced in the state to see its historical yield data.
